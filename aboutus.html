About

Top pack co. Established in 2016 in Kuwait, Top Pack Company has emerged as a prominent manufacturer and distributor of premium paper and plastic food packaging solutions. Committed to innovation, sustainability, and excellence, the company prioritizes quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction, aiming to provide packaging that not only enhances food presentation but also ensures safety and convenience.


Top Pack consistently invests in cutting-edge technologies and sustainable materials to offer clients reliable and cost-effective packaging options. The company boasts a skilled team provide innovative ideas, and strict quality assurance. Top Pack focuses intently on producing all goods to foster trust among clients, supported by a global network of suppliers.

With two manufacturing facilities in Kuwait, the company is devoted to delivering high-quality products, exceptional service, competitive pricing, and prompt delivery, thereby establishing long-term and robust relationships with customers in the region. Top Pack specializes in customized printed packaging, offering a wide array of tailored eco-friendly products designed to meet the specific needs of restaurants, cafes, bakeries, and food service businesses. Their product range providing high-quality and sustainable food packaging solutions.






Our Mission

At Top Pack we understand that packaging is the bridge between food manufacturers and consumers. It is our responsibility to ensure that this bridge is strong, reliable, and environmentally conscious. We continue to invest in research and development, adopting the latest technologies to create packaging solutions that are not only functional but also eco-friendly.

Our Vision

At Top Pack, our vision is to set the standard for innovation and quality in the disposable food packaging industry. Since our inception in 2016, we have dedicated ourselves to providing high-quality, customizable plastic solutions that meet the unique needs of our diverse customer base.
