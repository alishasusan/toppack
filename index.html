About Us

TOP PACK COMPANY FOR PAPER & PLASTIC PRODUCTS

We provide high-quality, eco-friendly packaging solutions designed to meet industry standards.

Top Pack Co. was established in 2016 in Kuwait and has become a leading manufacturer and distributor of premium paper and plastic food packaging solutions. With a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and excellence, we prioritize quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction.

We consistently invest in cutting-edge technologies and sustainable materials to offer cost-effective packaging solutions. Our skilled team focuses on quality assurance and building trust among our clients with a global network of suppliers.

With two manufacturing facilities in Kuwait, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality products, exceptional service, competitive pricing, and fast delivery.

We specialize in customized printed packaging for restaurants, cafes, bakeries, and food service businesses, ensuring sustainable food packaging solutions.
